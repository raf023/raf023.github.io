Glitch Collage

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: I searched online for some interesting pictures and came across images of graffiti. I thought it would be a great idea to create a cool collage. Using Photoshop, I'll randomly cut a small section of the image vertically with the intention of pasting it over the original image, creating a pattern. I then repeated this process until I reached the other end of the image.
