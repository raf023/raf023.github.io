Sound Design

Tool(s): Adobe Premiere

Project Description: We were tasked with watching short videos and choosing one that we would like to customize with sound. I chose one featuring a lot of dancing, either in space or at a club. I planned to use an online free sound website for this assignment. I added lots of footsteps and clothing rustling sounds to enhance the physical appeal of the video.
