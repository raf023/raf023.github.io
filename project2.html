Movie Poster

Tool(s): Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Stock

Project Description: We were tasked with reading three short stories that we will have to create movie posters for. I chose a short story called 'The Secret Life of Walter Mitty' by James Thurber. I used Adobe Illustrator to design my movie poster, with the idea of using his secret lives as different boxes. I used images from Adobe Stock to create the movie poster.
