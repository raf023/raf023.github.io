12 Months of 2020

Tool(s): Adobe Premiere, Youtube

Project Description: We were tasked with creating a 2-minute video that narrates a year, spanning 12 months with 10-second clips for each month, using clips from YouTube. I chose the year 2020 for this assignment because it had a significant impact on me and many other individuals. I used clips related to COVID, unemployment, George Floyd, and other events that occurred during that year.
