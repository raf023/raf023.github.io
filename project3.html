Creating a New Species

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Stock

Project Description: We were tasked with creating a new animal species composed of three different animals using Photoshop. I photoshopped a camel with a sheep's head, horse feet, and a lizard tail to create my animal. I also edited the colors of the different parts to white. I used Adobe Stock for the photos.
